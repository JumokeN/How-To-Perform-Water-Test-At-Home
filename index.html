How To Perform Water Test At Home

Safe Test
  1. Is the water clear?
  2. Is the water odorless?
  3. Is the water tasteless?
  4. Is the water cloudy?
  5. Do your hands feel slimy after washing them with soap and water?

It's important to perform all these tests as drinking contaminated water is detrimental to one's health.

Ensure that the water is clear, odorless, tasteless and not cloudy.

Inspect Water

Ensure there are no visible particles.

Fill a glass with water and inspect closely for any visible particles.

Cloudy Check

Let the glass of water rest for a few minutes.

Place glass close to a light source to check if it's cloudy. If water appears cloudy, do not drink it.

Color Check

Check the glass of water for any changes in color.

If the water is colored, even slightly, it indicates that it is contaminated. Do not drink colored water.

Smell Check

Place glass of water close to your nose and smell it.

If it has any odours, kindly discard and reach out to your water laboratory for additional tests on water quality.
